# Anti-AI Writing Protocol (Human-Status Encoding)
**Purpose:** Eliminate AI linguistic markers to increase trust and high-ticket conversion.
**Source:** Inspired by Conor Bronsdon's "Avoid AI Writing" framework.

## 1. Vocabulary Blacklist (The "De-bot" List)
*   **AVOID:** Leverage, Utilize, Seamless, Robust, Transformative, Pivotal, Comprehensive, Embark, Delve, Navigate.
*   **USE:** Use, Smooth, Strong, Change, Main, Start, Look at, Go through.

## 2. Structural Irregularity Rules
*   **Rhythm:** Break uniform sentence lengths. Use fragments. Lead with the punchline.
*   **Transitions:** Kill "Moreover," "Furthermore," and "In conclusion." Use "and," "also," or no transition at all.
*   **Copula Hygiene:** Avoid "serves as" or "boasts." Use "is" or "has."

## 3. The "Evidence vs. Inflation" Filter
*   Never tell the reader a project is "game-changing." Show the exact ROI (e.g., "+1M SAR Profit").
*   Replace "Experts believe" with specific names, dates, or data points.
*   Avoid "False Ranges" (e.g., "From villas to flights"). List the specific top 3 items instead.

## 4. Tone Governance
*   **Zero Politeness:** Remove "Certainly!", "I hope this helps," or "Let's explore."
*   **Direct Assertion:** State facts. Don't frame them with sycophantic preambles.
